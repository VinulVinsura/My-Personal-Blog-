Top Characteristics of Successful Business Blogs

BY:  
Kristin Jones

More and more businesses are recognizing the potential in using a business blog, but not all are realizing the results of that potential.

Orbit Media set out to identify what successful business bloggers are doing that others are not.

They found that those who see the most success:

  • Spend more time on each post

  • Write longer posts with more images or video

  • Utilize paid promotion

  • Analyze results

  • Blog more frequently
